Group Runs

Indy Runners Hub provides group club runs and walks for your year-round enjoyment. We typically have a great showing for our runs and there is a pace for the beginner, the flyer, and everyone in between. We have 3 mile and 6 mile runs and no one will be left behind.

Our runs will happen regardless of weather conditions (unless conditions are dangerous). Should a run be canceled, we will post it here on our site. Please check this page before leaving for the run.

We'd like to find a great south side running route, please let us know if you have one!

Other Run Groups

There are several Run Groups for you to explore and try if IndyRunnersHUB is not the right fit for you, no matter the reason. In fact as a beginner you may want to try a couple different run groups until you find what works best. The four additional groups I would like to mention are Pleasant Run Club, Speedway Running Club, Fishers Running Club, and Indy Runners. There are several other run groups out there. If you need additional help, reach out and a coach can point you in the right direction.

Pleasant Run Club

Pleasant Run Club is a new run group that meets for year-round weekly Wednesday evening runs at at Sense Charter School. The first run was established February 15, 2023 and they haven't stopped since. This group has runs for various distances and sometimes joins up with other run groups for double the fun. If you are in the Garfield Park or Fountain Square area we recommend giving this club strong consideration. Click the link to check them out!

Indy Runners

Indy Runners is a group that meets year-round for either runs or walks 6 days a week. They meet different days at Ft. Harrison, Broad RIpple, Downtown Indy, and Hinkle Fieldhouse. Their slogan is a place for every pace. The group began in existence since 1978 and they are a not-for-profit club. They offer a 30-day initial trial membership and after that you will need to pay a $35 or $70 membership fee for individuals or families respectively to participate. The club accomodates both walkers and runners and has a lot to offer. If you are in the Indianapolis area we recommend giving this club strong consideration. Click the link to check them out!

Speedway Running Club

Speedway Running Club is a group that meets for weekly Wednesday evening runs at the Daredevil. The run club began in 2015 and has been a 501(c)(3) organization since 2020. They do offer memberships for a $50 fee, but you don't have to be a member to participate in the runs. The club accomodates both walkers and runners. If you are in the Speedway area we recommend giving this club strong consideration. Click the link to check them out!

Fishers Running Club

Fishers Running Club is a group that meets at various locations in Fishers for both weekly Wednesday evening runs and Saturday morning runs. The run club began in 2017 and they are a not-for-profit volunteer organization. They have individual memberships that start at $40 yearly and family memberships that start at $100 yearly. You do not need to be a member to join runs however they do offer specific training only to members. The club accomodates both walkers and runners. If you are in the Fishers area we recommend giving this club strong consideration. Click the link to check them out!
